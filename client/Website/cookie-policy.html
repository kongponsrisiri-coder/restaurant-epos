Cookie Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how SiamEPOS uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit siamepos.co.uk.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
lang_preference Essential Remembers your language choice (English or Thai) 1 year
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand how visitors use our site (anonymous) 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes users 24 hours

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled. They do not track you across other websites.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. All data is anonymised and aggregated — we cannot identify individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at info@siamepos.co.uk.
