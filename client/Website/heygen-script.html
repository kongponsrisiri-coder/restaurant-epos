SiamEPOS HeyGen Script

AI Video Production Guide

Platform: HeyGen Duration: ~90 seconds Scenes: 8 Language: English (TH version too) Format: 16:9 landscape + 9:16 portrait

🎬 How to Use This in HeyGen

1

Create a new video in HeyGen → choose "AI Avatar" → pick your avatar (recommendations below)

2

Add 8 scenes (one per row below). Paste the script text into each scene's script box.

3

Set the background for each scene using the instructions in the "HeyGen Setup" column.

4

Add text overlays as specified. Use HeyGen's text element tool — position top or bottom.

5

Preview → Generate. Download in 1080p. Done!

6

For Thai version: Duplicate the project, swap script to Thai text, choose a Thai-voice avatar.

Professional male presenter, UK-style. Confident tone. Good for B2B pitch.

🎙️ Voice tip: In HeyGen, after selecting your avatar, click "Voice" and try different accents. For this video, a British English or neutral accent works best. Avoid strong American accents — your audience is in the UK.
