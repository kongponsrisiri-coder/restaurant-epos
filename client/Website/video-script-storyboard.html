Creative Brief

Objective

Convince Thai restaurant owners in the UK that SiamEPOS is the only EPOS built for them — and make booking a demo feel like the obvious next step.

Target Audience

Thai restaurant owners & managers in the UK, aged 30–55, often bilingual. Currently using multiple disconnected apps or a generic EPOS with no Thai support.

Tone

Warm, confident, modern. Not salesy — more like a friend who found something brilliant and wants to share it. Proud of Thai culture.

Distribution

Facebook & Instagram (paid + organic), YouTube pre-roll, siamepos.co.uk hero section, WhatsApp/Line outreach to prospects.

Music Direction

Act 1: tense, low percussion (problem energy) → Act 2 downbeat: one clear piano note (the moment of discovery) → Acts 3–5: upbeat modern Thai-fusion, confident and warm. BPM ~110. Royalty-free recommendation: Artlist or Epidemic Sound, search "Thai fusion upbeat corporate".

Screen Recordings

  • Table map in use (live data)

Social Cuts

  • 30s cut: Acts 2+3 only (features)

Voiceover Brief

  • Native Thai speaker, English accent
  • Warm, professional — not "ad voice"
  • Male or female (test both)
  • Record Thai version separately
  • Pace: conversational, not rushed
